meet ashley song

professional espresso tonic drinker. biased reviewer. usc communication senior. 21. oc/la resident. all glory to God!

why espresso tonics?

where did this hyperfixation come from? i tried my friend's espresso tonic once and immediately fell in love -- i love coffee and sparkling water so it was the perfect unique combination for me!

why did you start this ranking list?

i don't really remember ...... i just hyperfixated on espresso tonics and wanted to see how it varied for each coffee shop & which one tasted the best! i guess i just wrote down my thoughts in my notes app so that i wouldn't forget what they tasted like to me.

how can i contact you?

if you'd like to stay updated on my non-espresso tonic endeavors, check me out on instagram @ashleysongg! send me a dm of your favorite espresso tonic recommendation :D
