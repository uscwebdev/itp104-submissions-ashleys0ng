what is an espresso tonic?

an espresso tonic is the most delicious drink (my favorite drink). i like to describe it as a sparkling americano, as it's made with tonic water and an espresso shot. cafes typically add syrup to enhance the flavor and specialize their espresso tonic!

what is this blog about?

i have an entry in my iphone notes app where i write a review for every espresso tonic that i try -- this blog is to store each review in blog format :p
