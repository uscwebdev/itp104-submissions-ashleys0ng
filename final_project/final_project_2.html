the reviews

smoking tiger - 9.5/10

  1. PRICE: 6.0
  2. same price as stereo but the cup size is larger
  3. soo yummy and citrusy
  4. so close to my house!! so convenient

stereoscope - 8.9/10

  1. PRICE: 6.95
  2. perfect balance between sweet and espresso
  3. SO YUMMY AND REFRESHING
  4. very close to my house
  5. it is very expensive tho and kind of small. gone too quickly

homeboy coffee - 8/10

  1. PRICE: 6.0
  2. really good and citrus is yum
  3. i know it's probably just the ratio but it looks like they don't fill it up enough :')

the mugs - 8/10

  1. PRICE: 7.75
  2. it's basically 8.......
  3. really good but too much orange syrup... too sweet and overpowers the espresso taste

play coffee - 7.75/10

  1. PRICE: 7.0
  2. so expensive for why???? and size????
  3. but the drink is good very orange maybe a little too much but not a bad thing, little sweet

little ox coffee - 7.5/10

  1. PRICE: 6.5
  2. strong espresso flavor, light guava taste, pretty good but not the most impressive
  3. little pricey but i'd come here again!

holos coffee - 6/10

  1. PRICE: 6.25
  2. very cohesive taste
  3. too lemony i just did not like that
  4. didn't hate it, it was good but i didn't love it

enchanted coffee bar - 5.5/10

  1. PRICE: 6.0
  2. also kind of lemony and didn't like that
  3. same price as smoking tiger but i wouldn't drink it here over smoking tiger
