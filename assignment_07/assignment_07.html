Final Project Proposal

Topic

My chosen topic is Espresso Tonic Reviews.

Currently my favorite drink is an Espresso Tonic and I have an entry in my Notes app where I review each one that I try. For my final project, I plan to create a blog where I will post these reviews.

Audience

My intended audience is my friends and those who are also looking for the best espresso tonic!

Design & Style

An espresso tonic typically consists of two colors - espresso brown (for the drink) and orange (from the garnish - often a dried blood orange). I want the color scheme of my website to maintain these colors but also play with their complementary colors, such as various shades of pink, brown, and orange.
